Welcome! Strive Rite is a task manager for parents. It is proven that kids who do chores learn responsibility and gain important life skills that will serve them well throughout their lives.


Create a responsibilities chart to keep track of all the tasks for your children. Be sure to input a value for each task and Strive Rite will keep log your child's progress and earnings as they work toward some of their favorite things


Click the 'Get Started' button to begin setting up your chart.
